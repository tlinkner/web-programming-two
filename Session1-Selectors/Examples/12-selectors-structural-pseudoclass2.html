Structural Pseudo-Class Variations

selector:first-of-type — finds the first element matching the selector (to the left)

selector:nth-child(2) — finds the second element matching the selector (to the left)

selector:nth-child(3n) — finds every 3rd element matching the selector (to the left)

selector:last-child — finds the last element matching the selector (to the left)

selector:nth-child(odd) — finds every odd element matching the selector (to the left)
